Project Overview

"Developing a system that improves how product records and stock transactions are handled."

Process Analysis

Analyzes the current manual or semi-manual inventory process to find inefficiencies.

Business Rules

Identifies existing tracking rules used for product and stock lifecycle management.

Record Lifecycle

Highlights how records are created, updated, viewed, and deleted (CRUD).

Accuracy

Ensures accurate stock monitoring and high levels of operational accountability.

Problem Statement

Manual Challenges

  • • Inaccurate stock counts
  • • Difficulty tracking product movement
  • • Late detection of low-stock items
  • • Lack of audit trails

The Solution

A simple, reliable, and organized inventory system that manages products, tracks changes in real-time, and alerts users before stock-outs occur.

Application Description

Intended Users

Admin: Manages users, products, and settings.
Staff: Updates stock and views inventory reports.

Core Goals

Improve organizational efficiency through centralized record keeping and automated notifications.
